🎮 About CapsWars

⚡ Fast Gameplay

CapsWars is a game where you play agianst a bot to gain Elo. When your Elo is higher the bot gets better and better.

💥 Epic Collisions

Your caps can collide with your opponent's caps! Every collision can change the outcome of the game, making each match unique.

🏆 Goal

Move all three of your caps from your starting side to the opposite side before your opponent does.

📖 How To Play

1

The game begins with 3 caps on the left (your side) and 3 caps on the right (your opponent's side).

2
3

When caps collide, the race becomes even more exciting! Use quick reactions and smart timing to gain the advantage.

4

The first to successfully move all three caps to the opposite side wins the battle! 🎉
